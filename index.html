Calgary 311 Service Requests
— Civic Operations Analysis

End-to-end SQL analytics project on 6.67 million civic service records from the City of Calgary Open Data Portal. Designed a staging→analytics mart pipeline, built 30-day recurrence detection logic, and identified geographic hotspots to inform municipal resource allocation.

Processed 6,672,894 records across 4 modular SQL scripts (temporal, efficiency, geographic, trends)
~41–46% of resolved issues recur within 30 days — revealing systemic gaps in municipal maintenance
Identified Downtown, Beltline & Bowness as top hotspot communities by recurring incident density
Seasonal demand peaks in June (730k+ requests); Tuesday–Thursday see highest weekday volume
